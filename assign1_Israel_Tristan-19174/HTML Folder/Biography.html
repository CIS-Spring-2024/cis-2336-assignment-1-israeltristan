Cafeteria Biography

Israel Tristan

Israel is the proud new owner of Coogs Tavern, conveniently located in our UH at SugarLand Campus. As a recent graduate from UH, Israel has firsthand knowledge of the culture and preferences of our students and is committed to implementing necessary changes.

Israel's journey into cafeteria ownership began in the fast food industry, where he quickly rose to a managerial position at the age of 16. Over the years, he further advanced his skills in food preparation and daily operations management. Encouraged by his experiences, Israel pursued a degree in Hospitality Management, equipping him with the expertise needed to usher in a fresh chapter for The Coogs Tavern.

Beyond overseeing the cafeteria's operations, Israel actively engages with the community through initiatives such as food drives and local park clean-ups. His commitment to giving back comes from all the support he recieved from his family and from other coogs.

When he's not at the Coogs Tavern, Israel enjoys spending quality time with friends and staying aheadof emerging trends through social media research.
